Lasagna Recipe

Stacked layers of pasta sheets alternating with ragu, bechamel sauce and cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of Lasagna Sheets
  • 500g Ragu
  • 150g Bechamel
  • 100g Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Spoon one third of the meat sauce into the dish, then cover with lasagna sheets. Drizzle over a few ladels of bechamel sauce.
  2. Repeat this process until you have three layers of lasagna sheets in the dish. Cover with the remaining bechamel sauce, completely covering any visible pasta.
  3. Scatter Mozarella cheese over the top.
  4. Cover with foil and bake in the oven for 30 minutes.
  5. Remove the foil from the dish and cook for a further 10 minutes.
