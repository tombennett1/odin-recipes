Pizza Recipe

Pizza is a flattended disk of bread dough topped with tomato sauce and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 200g Pizza Dough
  • 300ml Tomato Sauce
  • 80g Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Kneed the dough into a circle roughly 30cm in diameter
  2. Liberally cover with the tomato sauce, making sure to cover all of the surface.
  3. Lay the mozzarella cheese on top, being careful not to leave gaps.
  4. Bake in the oven until the cheese is golden brown
  5. Cut into 8 slices and serve
